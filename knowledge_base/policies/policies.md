# VillageTaste Resort Policies

To ensure a serene, respectful, and ecologically sound environment for all our guests, please read our resort policies and guidelines.

---

## 1. Check-In Policy

*   **Standard Check-In Time**: Starts from **2:00 PM** onwards.
*   **Documentation**: All guests (including children) must provide valid government-issued photo identification (Passport, Driving License, or National ID) at the time of check-in. Foreign nationals must present a valid Visa and Passport.
*   **Early Check-In**: Subject to room availability and must be requested at least 24 hours in advance. Early check-in before 8:00 AM incurs a charge equivalent to 100% of the daily rate; check-in between 8:00 AM and 2:00 PM is charged at 50% of the daily rate.
*   **Key Collection**: Physical key cards will be issued upon completion of registration and secure deposit registration at the front desk.

---

## 2. Check-Out Policy

*   **Standard Check-Out Time**: By **11:00 AM**.
*   **Late Check-Out**: Subject to availability and must be requested at the reception desk.
    *   Late check-out between 11:00 AM and 3:00 PM incurs a fee equal to 50% of the daily room rate.
    *   Late check-out after 3:00 PM incurs a full day's room charge.
*   **Folio Settlement**: Guests must review and settle their final billing folio (including room charges, dining fees, activities, and spa services) at the reception. Folio confirmations are emailed via our automated system.
*   **Luggage Storage**: Complimentary luggage storage is available at the lobby desk for guests wishing to explore the grounds post-checkout.

---

## 3. Cancellation & Booking Modification Policy

*   **Standard Season Bookings**:
    *   Cancellations made **7 days or more** before the scheduled check-in date: Eligible for a full refund minus a 5% administrative processing fee.
    *   Cancellations made **between 3 and 7 days** before the check-in date: 50% of the total booking value will be charged as a cancellation fee.
    *   Cancellations made **within 72 hours** of check-in, or No-Shows: 100% of the total booking value will be charged.
*   **Peak Season Bookings (December 15 – January 15)**:
    *   Cancellations must be made **30 days or more** before the scheduled check-in date for a refund. Cancellations made within 30 days are subject to a 100% cancellation fee.
*   **Booking Modifications**: Subject to availability. Requesting date modifications 7 days or more prior to arrival is free of charge; within 7 days, it is treated as a cancellation and rebooking.

---

## 4. Refund Policy

*   **Refund Eligibility**: Approved refunds are issued only for cancellations that comply with our Cancellation Policy.
*   **Processing Time**: Refunds are initiated automatically and take **5 to 7 business days** to reflect in the guest's account.
*   **Refund Method**: All refunds are processed back to the original credit card, bank account, or digital wallet used during the booking. Cash refunds are not permitted under any circumstances.
*   **Administrative Fee**: A non-refundable 5% administrative fee applies to all processed refunds to cover banking transaction charges.

---

## 5. Quiet Hours & Digital Detox Policy

*   **Quiet Hours**: Observed daily from **10:00 PM to 7:00 AM**.
*   **Loudspeakers & Noise**: Playing music on external loudspeakers on verandas, lawns, or balconies is strictly prohibited during quiet hours. Guests are requested to keep TV and device volumes low.
*   **Digital Detox Zones**: To preserve the peaceful soundscape and encourage mindfulness, guest accommodation zones (Standard Cottages, Deluxe Villas, Luxury Suites) are WiFi-free. Free high-speed WiFi is available only in the Central Village Square. Portable hotspots for rooms are available at the front desk upon request.

---

## 6. Pet Policy

*   **Designated Accommodations**: Pets (dogs and cats only) are welcome in our **Deluxe Villas only**.
*   **Exclusions**: Pets are strictly not allowed in Standard Cottages or Luxury Suites to avoid disrupting nearby native birds and wildlife nesting zones.
*   **Pet Fee**: A charge of **$25 per pet per night** applies (maximum of 2 pets per villa).
*   **Public Areas**: Pets must be kept on a leash at all times in public spaces. Pets are restricted from entering the dining hall (The Harvest Table) and the natural swimming pool areas.
*   **Behavior**: Owners are responsible for cleaning up after their pets and ensuring they do not disturb other guests.

---

## 7. Safety & Emergency Policy

*   **Trail Regulations**: Guided nature hikes and trails require sturdy enclosed walking shoes. Guests must remain on the designated trails at all times.
*   **First-Aid & Medical**: A designated first-aid center is located in the Village Square. An on-call nurse is available 24/7.
*   **Fire Safety**: Bonfires and campfires are permitted only in designated fire pits under resort staff supervision. Unsupervised fire building is strictly prohibited.
*   **Child Supervision**: Children under the age of 12 must be supervised by an adult at all times, especially near the stream, Lotus Lake, and natural pool.

---

## 8. Sustainability & Plastic-Free Policy

*   **Single-Use Plastic Ban**: VillageTaste Resort is a **single-use plastic-free zone**. Guests must not bring plastic water bottles, disposable shopping bags, or styrofoam packaging onto the property.
*   **Water Conservation**: Guests are encouraged to conserve water. Shower linens and bedding are changed every third day of stay unless a daily change is explicitly requested via the green housekeeping card.
*   **Waste Segregation**: Trash bins in accommodations are split for dry recyclable waste and wet organic waste. Organic waste is processed in our on-site composting unit.
*   **Energy Conservation**: Rooms feature master switches that turn off all lights and fans automatically when keys are removed. Please conserve energy by switching off appliances when not in use.
*   **Smoking Policy**: All indoor spaces are 100% smoke-free. Guests may smoke in designated outdoor smoking zones only. A $250 cleaning fee will be charged for violations.
