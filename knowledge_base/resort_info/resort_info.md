# VillageTaste Resort Profile & Information

Welcome to **VillageTaste Resort**, an eco-luxury village-themed destination nestled in the foothills of the Whispering Hills. Our resort is designed to recreate the simple charm, peaceful pace, and organic lifestyle of a traditional countryside village, combined with premium hospitality and modern comfort.

---

## 1. Resort History & Vision

Founded in 2018 by local conservationists Devendra and Sarita Patel, VillageTaste Resort began as a project to restore 25 acres of barren, over-farmed land. The founders envisioned a sanctuary where travelers could disconnect from fast-paced urban lives and experience authentic rural heritage. Over three years, they planted 2,000 native fruit trees, constructed rainwater checkdams, and built the resort using traditional architectural styles. Today, the resort stands as a model for regenerative tourism and supports over 40 families from neighboring villages through employment and handicraft partnerships.

---

## 2. The Village Theme & Architecture

Our architecture pays homage to the traditional mud houses of rural Gujarat. 
*   **Traditional Materials**: The buildings feature hand-packed mud walls mixed with agricultural husks, which provide excellent thermal insulation.
*   **Handmade Artistry**: The walls are decorated with Lipan Kaam (traditional clay and mirror relief work) and Warli paintings hand-drawn by local tribal women.
*   **Clay Tiling**: Roofs are tiled with country clay half-tiles hand-thrown by local potters, and supported by reclaimed teakwood beams.
*   **Clay Ovens**: The kitchen uses traditional wood-fired stoves (chulhas) and clay tandoor ovens, maintaining historical cooking methods.

---

## 3. Resort Layout & Hamlets

*   **The Village Square (Central Hub)**: Houses the reception lobby, administrative office, main dining hall (The Harvest Table), Craft Pavilion, and boutique store.
*   **Cottage Hamlet (North Zone)**: A cluster of 15 Standard Cottages, designed with straw thatch structures and organic mud plaster.
*   **The Orchard Meadow (West Zone)**: Surrounds the central organic farm and holds 10 Deluxe Villas featuring private backyard fruit gardens.
*   **Whispering Stream Hamlet (East Zone)**: Home to 5 Luxury Suites built directly over or alongside the running freshwater stream.
*   **Activity Pasture (South Zone)**: Houses the Pottery Shed, Organic Farm learning plots, Organic Dairy Farm, and the start of the hiking trailheads.

## 4. Accommodation Types

We offer three premium classes of accommodation:

### A. Standard Cottage
*   **Capacity**: Up to 2 Adults + 1 Child.
*   **Description**: Traditional round-house design (bhanga style) with hand-painted mud walls and thatched roofs.
*   **Amenities**: King-sized bed, en-suite bathroom with natural stone shower, private veranda overlooking the garden, writing desk, ceiling fan ventilation, and hot-water bags.

### B. Deluxe Villa
*   **Capacity**: Up to 4 Adults.
*   **Description**: Spacious layouts featuring separate living and sleeping areas, a private courtyard with a traditional wooden swing, and views of the fruit orchards.
*   **Amenities**: All Standard Cottage amenities + premium organic tea station, outdoor rain shower, private bonfire fire-pit area, and ceiling fan ventilation (no air conditioning to maintain the traditional eco-concept).

### C. Luxury Suite
*   **Capacity**: Up to 4 Adults + 2 Children.
*   **Description**: High-end stilt cottages bordering the mountain stream, featuring custom local art pieces and a private wooden deck extending over the water.
*   **Amenities**: All Deluxe Villa amenities + climate-control air conditioning and electric heating, private deep plunge pool, copper bathtub, 24/7 dedicated butler service, and a complimentary organic breakfast basket.

## 5. Resort Facilities

*   **The Harvest Table Restaurant**: Serving 100% farm-to-table organic breakfast, lunch, and dinner. It features open-air dining looking out over the farms.
*   **The Village Spa**: Offering traditional Ayurvedic oil massages, herbal steam therapy, and hot stone baths led by licensed therapists.
*   **Boutique Shop**: Sells hand-woven fabrics, organic spices, local honey, and terracotta pottery created by village craftsmen.
*   **Natural Swimming Pool**: A chemical-free pool filled with fresh spring water and filtered using natural aquatic plants.
*   **Organic Dairy Farm**: Home to local Gir cows, offering guests interactive dairy farming experiences.

---

## 6. Sustainability Initiatives

*   **Solar Power Grid**: 100% of our daytime electricity is generated by our on-site solar panels.
*   **Water Conservation**: Rainwater harvesting structures collect over 1.2 million liters of water annually, recharging local groundwater.
*   **Zero-Waste Kitchen**: All organic kitchen waste is composted and used as fertilizer in our organic farm. Single-use plastic is strictly banned.
*   **Linen Conservation**: Bed linens are changed every third day to minimize water and detergent usage.
*   **Local Sourcing**: All restaurant ingredients are sourced within a 10km radius, minimizing carbon emissions from transport.

---

## 7. Guest Experiences & Integrations

*   **Farming Workshops**: Hands-on learning of organic composting, seed sowing, and crop harvesting.
*   **Local Artisan Workshops**: Interactive terracotta pottery making and handloom weaving sessions.
*   **Community Integration**: Weekly guided tours to the neighboring village of Sundarpora, where a portion of the tour fees directly funds the local primary school.
*   **Folk Interactions**: Nightly bonfire gatherings with storytelling, acoustic folk music, and traditional Gujarati dances like Garba.
