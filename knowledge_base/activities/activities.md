# VillageTaste Resort Activities & Experiences

At VillageTaste Resort, we encourage guests to reconnect with nature, traditional crafts, and rural culture. Below is our catalog of experiences, workshops, and outdoor activities available on the property.

---

## 1. Craft & Learning Workshops

### A. Terracotta Pottery Workshop
*   **Location**: The Pottery Shed (Activity Pasture)
*   **Schedule**: Daily from 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM and 3:00 PM – 5:00 PM.
*   **Description**: Work alongside master artisans from local villages. Learn to throw clay on a traditional potter's wheel, shape cups, bowls, or lamps, and fire them in our wood kiln. Take your creation home as a souvenir.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary for Deluxe Villa and Luxury Suite guests. $15 per session for Standard Cottage guests (materials included).
*   **Booking**: Requires reservation at least 2 hours in advance at the reception desk.

### B. Organic Farming & Harvesting Session
*   **Location**: Learning Plots (Orchard Meadow / Organic Farm)
*   **Schedule**: Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays from 7:30 AM – 9:00 AM.
*   **Description**: Get your hands in the soil! Learn composting techniques, organic pest control, and harvest fresh greens, herbs, and fruits to be cooked at the resort restaurant. Guided by our head horticulturist.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary for all registered guests.
*   **Booking**: Walk-ins welcome; register at the farm gate 10 minutes prior.

### C. Traditional Weaver's Loom Demonstration
*   **Location**: Craft Pavilion (Village Square)
*   **Schedule**: Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays from 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM.
*   **Description**: Witness local weavers create beautiful handloom textiles using traditional wooden looms. Guests can try their hand at thread winding and basic shuttle weaving under expert guidance.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary demonstration. Hands-on workshop: $10 per person.
*   **Booking**: Register at the Craft Pavilion.

## 2. Culinary Experiences

### D. Traditional Clay-Pot Cooking Class
*   **Location**: The Community Kitchen (Village Square)
*   **Schedule**: Daily from 11:30 AM – 1:30 PM (prepares lunch).
*   **Description**: Cook traditional Kathiyawadi and Gujarati dishes on wood-fired clay stoves (chulhas). Learn to make hand-rolled millet rotlas, slow-cooked curries in earthen pots, and smoked buttermilk.
*   **Fee**: $30 per person (includes the multi-course lunch you prepare).
*   **Booking**: Required 24 hours in advance. Maximum 8 participants per session.

### E. Dairy Farm & Cow-Milking Experience
*   **Location**: Organic Dairy Farm (South Zone)
*   **Schedule**: Daily from 6:30 AM – 7:30 AM and 5:00 PM – 6:00 PM.
*   **Description**: Experience life on a rural dairy farm. Learn about cattle care, feed the calves, and try traditional cow-milking. Taste fresh, warm milk boiled with cardamom.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary for all guests.
*   **Booking**: Drop-in at the Dairy Farm gate.

## 3. Wellness & Nature Excursions

### F. Sunrise Yoga & Guided Meditation
*   **Location**: The Stream Deck (East Zone)
*   **Schedule**: Every morning from 6:00 AM – 7:15 AM.
*   **Description**: Start your day with gentle yoga postures and mindfulness meditation listening to the sounds of flow and forest birds. Suitable for beginners and experts alike. Yoga mats provided.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary for all guests.
*   **Booking**: Walk-ins welcome.

### G. Whispering Hills Hiking Trail
*   **Location**: Trailhead starts at the south side of the Activity Pasture.
*   **Schedule**: Guided hikes leave daily at 8:00 AM and 4:00 PM.
*   **Description**: A moderate 4.5km circular trail led by our resort naturalist. Observe regional flora, birds, and enjoy a panoramic view of the valley from the Sunset Crag.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary.
*   **Safety Notice**: Sturdy walking shoes are required. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
*   **Booking**: Sign up at the front desk before 7:00 AM for morning hike, or 3:00 PM for afternoon hike.

### H. Bird Watching & Nature Walks
*   **Location**: East Marsh and Stream Trail (East Zone)
*   **Schedule**: Daily from 6:30 AM – 8:00 AM.
*   **Description**: Explore the diverse local ecosystems of Whispering Hills. Spot migratory and resident birds like parakeets, sunbirds, and the Indian roller. Binoculars and field guides are provided.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary for all guests.
*   **Booking**: Sign up at the front desk by 8:00 PM the night before.

### I. Lake Fishing & Cane-Rod Angling
*   **Location**: Lotus Lake (North-East border)
*   **Schedule**: Daily from 9:00 AM – 11:30 AM and 3:30 PM – 6:00 PM.
*   **Description**: Enjoy relaxing catch-and-release fishing on Lotus Lake. We provide traditional bamboo cane rods, organic bait, and instructions for beginners.
*   **Fee**: $12 per rod kit. Free for Luxury Suite guests.
*   **Booking**: Direct rental at the Lake Boathouse.

### J. Rural Cycling & Farm Trail Rides
*   **Location**: Cycle Station (Village Square)
*   **Schedule**: Daily from 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM (self-guided).
*   **Description**: Explore the resort's 25 acres and the surrounding village trails on our geared mountain bikes. Maps of local trails are provided.
*   **Fee**: Free for the first 2 hours; $5 per hour thereafter.
*   **Booking**: Pick up a bike at the Cycle Station.

### K. Ayurvedic Herbal Garden Walk & Tea Tasting
*   **Location**: Medicinal Herb Garden (West Zone)
*   **Schedule**: Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Sundays from 4:30 PM – 5:30 PM.
*   **Description**: Learn about traditional Indian medicine (Ayurveda). Identify over 50 varieties of healing herbs and plants, followed by a tasting session of custom herbal teas.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary for all guests.
*   **Booking**: Sign up at the Village Spa.

## 4. Evening Gatherings & Culture

### L. Campfire Storytelling & Bonfire Night
*   **Location**: The Central Firepit (Village Square)
*   **Schedule**: Every evening from 7:30 PM – 9:30 PM (weather permitting).
*   **Description**: Gather around a roasting fire to listen to local folk legends told by village elders, accompanied by traditional acoustic instruments. Complimentary roasted sweet potatoes and hot spiced tea are served.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary for all guests.
*   **Booking**: Drop-in.

### M. Folk Dance Workshop: Garba & Dandiya
*   **Location**: Central Courtyard (Village Square)
*   **Schedule**: Saturdays and Sundays from 6:30 PM – 7:30 PM.
*   **Description**: Learn the basic steps of Garba and Dandiya, traditional folk dances of Gujarat. Colorful sticks (dandiyas) are provided. Dress in local attire is encouraged.
*   **Fee**: Complimentary for all guests.
*   **Booking**: Walk-in.

### N. Village Heritage Walking Tour
*   **Location**: Departs from the Reception Lobby.
*   **Schedule**: Wednesdays and Saturdays at 9:30 AM.
*   **Description**: Take a guided walk to the adjacent village of Sundarpora. Meet local artisans, visit a traditional village school, see historical stepwells, and learn about rural lifestyle.
*   **Fee**: $15 per person (proceeds support the village school).
*   **Booking**: Required 12 hours in advance at the reception desk.

## 5. Seasonal Activities

### O. Seasonal Mango Picking Excursion (Summer)
*   **Location**: The Kesar Mango Orchard (North Zone)
*   **Schedule**: Daily from 8:30 AM – 10:30 AM (Available March 15 to June 15).
*   **Description**: Join our harvesting team during the peak mango season. Pluck ripe organic Kesar mangoes from the trees and enjoy fresh mango slices right in the orchard. Take home a basket.
*   **Fee**: $20 per basket picked.
*   **Booking**: Inquire at the front desk.

### P. Seasonal Kite Flying Festival & Competition (Winter)
*   **Location**: Sunset Hill Lawn (South-West corner)
*   **Schedule**: Daily from 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM (Available November 1 to January 31).
*   **Description**: Experience the Gujarati tradition of kite flying (Uttarayan style). Learn kite-assembling, string-handling, and participate in friendly kite fighting matches with other guests.
*   **Fee**: $8 for a kit of 5 kites and spools.
*   **Booking**: Drop-in at the Sunset Hill Lawn.
