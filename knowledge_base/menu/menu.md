# The Harvest Table - Organic Dining Menu

This menu lists all the organic food and farm-fresh dishes served at the VillageTaste Resort restaurant. If you are wondering what food is served in the resort, we offer a complete selection of breakfast, lunch, dinner, desserts, beverages, and traditional Gujarati and village-special meals.

At **The Harvest Table**, our primary culinary philosophy is "Seed to Plate." Over 80% of our ingredients are harvested daily from the VillageTaste organic gardens, with the remainder sourced from certified local family farms within a 10km radius. We use cold-pressed wood oils (groundnut and mustard) and cook on traditional wood and clay stoves (chulhas) to bring out authentic flavors.

*   **Hand-Beaten Masala Poha ($10)**: Traditional steamed beaten rice flaked with onions, potatoes, mustard seeds, curry leaves, and roasted peanuts, finished with pomegranate seeds and grated fresh coconut.

*   **Steamed Dhokla Basket ($11)**: Soft, spongy fermented chickpea flour cakes tempered with mustard seeds, sesame seeds, green chilies, and coriander, served with green chutney (Gluten-Free, Vegan).
*   **Spiced Ragi Porridge ($10)**: Warm finger-millet porridge sweetened with organic jaggery, flavored with green cardamom, and topped with toasted flaxseeds.

## 2. Lunch & Dinner Menu (Served 12:30 PM – 3:30 PM & 6:30 PM – 10:00 PM)

### Soups & Starters
*   **Wild Mushroom & Barley Stew ($14)**: Earthy forest mushrooms simmered with pearl barley, fresh parsley, and vegetable broth.
*   **Crispy Herb Fritters ($15)**: Assorted seasonal farm greens and chickpea flour fritters, lightly spiced and served with sweet tamarind dip (Bhajiya).
*   **Harvest Garden Salad ($16)**: Crisp heirloom lettuce, radishes, carrots, cucumbers, and sunflower seeds dressed in a light wild-berry vinaigrette.
*   **Steamed Patra ($12)**: Colocasia leaves coated with a spiced gram flour paste, rolled, steamed, and pan-sautéed with mustard seeds and coconut shavings (Kathiyawadi style).
*   **Clay-Oven Spiced Khandvi ($12)**: Delicate rolls of cooked gram flour and yogurt batter, seasoned with ginger and green chilies, garnished with fresh coriander.
*   **Roasted Beetroot & Lentil Soup ($13)**: Smooth soup of fire-roasted garden beetroots and red lentils, flavored with roasted cumin.

*   **Clay Pot Stew ($24)**: Traditional slow-cooked seasonal vegetables and local legumes in a spiced gravy, served with hand-rolled flatbreads or brown rice (Vegan).

*   **Undhiyu ($26)**: Traditional Gujarati winter special of mixed vegetables (surti papdi, purple yam, sweet potato, raw banana, brinjal) and fried fenugreek dumplings (muthias) slow-cooked upside-down in clay pots.

*   **Dal Dhokli ($20)**: Spiced whole-wheat ribbon pasta cooked directly in a boiling sweet-and-sour split pigeon pea lentil soup, garnished with peanuts and coriander.
