# VillageTaste Resort FAQs

Here are answers to the most common questions asked by our guests.

---

## 1. Transportation & Location

### Q: How do I get to VillageTaste Resort?
**A**: The resort is located in the foothill valley, 85km from the nearest International Airport (Windsong Airport) and 20km from the Whispering Hills Railway Station. We provide private airport pickups starting at $60 and free shuttle runs to/from the railway station twice daily (at 9:00 AM and 3:00 PM). Please coordinate with the Reception Agent to book your shuttle seat.

### Q: Is there free parking available on-site?
**A**: Yes. We offer complimentary, secure on-site parking for all registered guests. Valet parking service is also available at no additional charge. We have designated charging ports for electric vehicles (EVs) in our parking zone.

---

## 2. Weather & What to Pack

### Q: What is the best season to visit?
**A**: The resort has pleasant weather year-round.
*   **Spring/Summer (March to June)**: Perfect for outdoor agricultural harvesting and swimming (temperatures range from 20°C to 32°C).
*   **Monsoon (July to September)**: The hills are vibrant green and streams flow high. Great for nature lovers.
*   **Winter (October to February)**: Mild days and cool nights (ranges from 8°C to 20°C). Perfect for bonfire storytelling.

### Q: Do the rooms have Air Conditioning and Heating?
**A**: Our Luxury Suites include climate control air conditioning and electric heaters. Standard Cottages and Deluxe Villas do not have active AC to align with our eco-conscious architecture, but they are built with double mud insulation and clay roof tiles that naturally keep rooms cool in summer and warm in winter. All rooms have ceiling fans and hot-water bags.

---

## 3. Bookings, Cancellations & Refunds

### Q: What is the booking reservation process?
**A**: Reservations can be made through our local Booking Agent. A booking request must include the guest's name, valid contact email, check-in and check-out dates, room type preference, and the total headcount of guests. You will receive an automated email confirmation upon successful reservation.

### Q: What is the cancellation policy?
**A**: For standard bookings, cancellations made 7 days or more before check-in receive a full refund minus a 5% administrative fee. Cancellations made between 3 and 7 days prior to check-in incur a 50% charge. No refunds are provided for cancellations made within 72 hours of arrival. During peak season (December 15 – January 15), cancellations must be made 30 days in advance to receive a refund.

### Q: How and when are refunds processed?
**A**: Approved refunds are processed automatically back to the original payment method. The processing timeline is 5 to 7 business days from the cancellation date. You will receive a receipt of the refund via email.

### Q: What are the check-in and check-out times?
**A**: Standard check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out must be requested 24 hours in advance and is subject to availability. Late check-out between 11:00 AM and 3:00 PM incurs a fee equal to 50% of the room rate.

---

## 4. Payments

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A**: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and local digital wallet systems. Cash is accepted at the reception, but we encourage contactless payments.

---

## 5. Resort Amenities & WiFi

### Q: Is WiFi available at the resort?
**A**: Yes. Free high-speed WiFi is available in the public areas (the Central Village Square and reception lobby). In order to encourage digital detox and mindfulness, accommodation hamlets (Standard Cottages, Deluxe Villas, Luxury Suites) do not have WiFi by default. However, portable hotspots can be provided upon request at the front desk.

### Q: Do you have a swimming pool?
**A**: Yes, we have a natural freshwater pool located near the Whispering Stream. It is chemical-free and filtered using aquatic plants and bio-filters. It is open from 6:30 AM to 6:30 PM daily.

---

## 6. Pets & Children

### Q: What is the pet policy at the resort?
**A**: We welcome dogs and cats in our Deluxe Villas only, subject to a fee of $25 per pet per night (maximum 2 pets per villa). Pets are not allowed in Standard Cottages or Luxury Suites due to wildlife nesting areas nearby. Pets must be kept on a leash in public spaces and are restricted from the dining hall and pool areas.

### Q: Is there a child policy for activities?
**A**: Children of all ages are welcome at the resort. Children under the age of 5 stay free of charge using existing bedding. Most activities, like the Petting Zoo and Organic Farm walk, are child-friendly. The Pottery workshop is suitable for children aged 6 and above.

---

## 7. Dining & Food

### Q: What dining options are available, and do you cater to special diets?
**A**: Our restaurant, The Harvest Table, serves organic farm-to-table breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Over 80% of our ingredients are sourced directly from our on-site organic farm. Please notify us of any food allergies during check-in.

### Q: Can we participate in crop harvesting?
**A**: Yes! Guests can join our horticulture team during the morning farming and harvesting sessions (Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays from 7:30 AM to 9:00 AM). You can pluck fresh herbs, greens, and vegetables that are later cooked at the restaurant.

---

## 8. Activities & Local Sightseeing

### Q: Do activities require advanced booking?
**A**: Yes, certain workshops like the Clay-Pot Cooking Class (24-hour notice) and Terracotta Pottery Workshop (2-hour notice) require prior booking. Outdoor excursions like the hiking trails, morning yoga, and bonfire gatherings are open to all guests without reservations.

### Q: Can we purchase pottery made during the workshop?
**A**: Absolutely. The terracotta items you shape during the workshop are fired in our kiln. Once cooled, you can collect and keep them at no extra charge. Additional handicrafts made by local artisans are available for purchase in our boutique shop.

---

## 9. Safety & Health

### Q: Do you have medical assistance on site?
**A**: We have a designated first-aid center and an on-call nurse available 24/7. The nearest fully-equipped municipal hospital is located in the town center, 20km away.

### Q: Are there safety precautions for the hiking trails?
**A**: Yes. Sturdy walking shoes are required for all hikes. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Guests are advised to stay on designated trails and carry spring water in the glass bottles provided.

---

## 10. Sustainability

### Q: What sustainability practices do you follow?
**A**: VillageTaste is built on eco-luxury principles. We operate on a solar power grid, use rainwater harvesting, run a zero-waste organic composting program, enforce a single-use plastic ban, and utilize local sustainable building materials like mud and bamboo.

### Q: Is smoking allowed on the property?
**A**: All indoor spaces, including cottages, villas, and suites, are strictly non-smoking. Guests may smoke in designated outdoor smoking zones only. A $250 cleaning fee is charged for smoking violations indoors.
